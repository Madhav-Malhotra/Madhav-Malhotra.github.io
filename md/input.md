### What I Learned
- I've been focusing a lot on relearning good habits. Exercising consistently by biking 10km/day, training and testing myself on my ability to stay calm under pressure/frustration, getting over social anxieties and introducing myself to strangers, and so on.
- It's been **helpful to be a little aloof in modern life**. Ex: Occasionally watching the news once a week instead of day to day => less anxiety, more irrelevant details ignored. Ex: Not getting too hung up over any one person or social interaction => more attempts to put myself out there, more diverse set of relationships. Ex: Not focusing too much on any one job application => keeping consistent perseverance/effort in the job hunt. 
- **The greatest joys in life are always shared :-)** I haven't found any richness like happy social connections and I haven't found any misery that the continuous pursuit of wealth can fully stave off.
