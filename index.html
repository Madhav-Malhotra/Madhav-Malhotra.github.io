About Me

I am a 16-year-old exploring emerging scientific and technological fields including artificial intelligence, nanotechnology, and renewable energy.


Some of my recent work includes consulting with Walmart Canada to decrease operational costs and consulting with Wealthsimple to improve customer acquisition. In addition, I am personally developing machine learning projects and publishing articles and other media on the field of artificial intelligence. My amazing mentors are experienced in founding and advising startups, allowing me to learn about the commercial applications of groundbreaking technologies to solve major world issues. Currently, I am continuing my work with artificial intelligence, amongst other extracurriculars projects, and pursuing an International Baccalaureate Diploma.

EXTRACURRICULARS

Volunteering

Leader - Recycling Club

I've worked at my school's recycling program for years. Every week, I direct volunteers in managing our school's waste to reduce our environmental impact. I've also created media campaigns on environmental stewardship and won the Tim Walker Memorial Award to renovate our program.

Guide - Anne Frank Exhibition

Anne Frank: A History for Today is a global exhibition to educate on the history of the Holocaust and how it is still relevant today. When it came to my school from across the world, I took part as a guide for students and the public, educating visitors on Anne Frank's story and its importance today.

Youth Writer - Kitchener Public Library

I work with the KPL to create content and activities for youth in my community. This ranges from writing reviews for movies, music, and other media to organising competitions and free community events available to all youth. I've also worked with the KPL to sponsor a community fundraiser in partnership with WAYVE.

Roots and Shoots

This charitable organisation at my school creates fundraisers and promotion campaigns for social and environmental causes. I've helped create fundraisers for mental health awareness, homeless youth, and pollinator protection among other causes and have launched an independent campaign to support rhino conservation and human rights documentation.
