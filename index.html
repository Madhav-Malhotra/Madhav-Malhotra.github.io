I'm a 17-year-old developer, designer, & Entrepreneur-in-training

My vision is to solve big problems in the world to leave behind a lasting legacy! Recently, I've been exploring exponential technologies like artificial intelligence and renewable energy. And on the side, I'm always learning more about modern philosophies, technical research, consulting for companies, and whatever else I can get my hands on. I also go to high school sometimes... 😉

Currently, I'm a cofounder at The Plastic Shift (a project to promote informed action to end plastic pollution) and The Knowledge Archives (a podcast to interview researchers about the latest discoveries in academia). I'm also an alumni from The Knowledge Society, where I've been learning how to join the next generation of business leaders! I'd love to hear from others to learn more about living intentionally and working to launch impactful ideas!
